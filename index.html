It was a dark and stormy night ...

when my foot walked away from my body

it traisped into the closet, rustling through the clutter.

Suddenly, my toes stretched and wrapped themselves around my favorite pair of shoes.

To an ordinary person, these shoes are just plain ordinary. Black leather boots with a wide berth for my toes, a high arch for my flat feet, and conforming shin-high sheath. My foot loves these shoes. Now why did it want them on this night? Why did it disembody itself for those shoes?
